Games we Play

Astroneer

A space exploration game based around trying to survive in harsh environments. You must make your fortune from exploring planets for the rarest of resources and and riches. Take on hostile environments either by yourself, or with a team and set up a base to fuel your efforts.

Developed by System Era Softworks

Steep

An extreme snow-sports game that lets you carve your own path. Whether you want to Ski, Snowboard, Wingsuit or Handglide, you can take on the mountains your own way. The option for races, time trials and score trials are all available if you want to feed your adrenaline cravings but there is also the option for free rides.

Developed by Ubisoft Annecy

Killing Floor 2

Filled with blood, guts and guns. Killing Floor 2 is about holding off waves of unrelenting zombie-esqe creatures known as 'zeds' any way you can. Recommended for cooperative play, you can take on your enemies with a number of specialities, perks and weapons.

Developed by Tripwire Interactive
