Welcome to Snack Attack

Welcome to the home page for the curiously named 'Snack Attack' gaming clan/group/whatever. From this hub, members and newcomers alike can check out our Twitch Channels, meet the crew, discover the games we play and information on how to access our Discord channel.

We're friendly (well...mostly), approachable (debatable) and are always looking for new members to join us in our antics! Whatever your favourite genre or game, we're usually up for a session. Hit us up on Discord to find out more! (LINK ABOVE)

So Who Are We?

Formed during a tense session of Rainbow Six: Siege, the name and idea to form a group was prompted by what can only be described as either pure brilliance or sheer madness.

The name, coined by none other than David (Mr. Flibble), has been our humble title ever since that fateful night and our UPlay tags now all bare a relationship to the peculiar name (CorporalCustard, LadyLettuce, SergeantSauce, you get the idea). We are a group of people coming from all over the world that share the same common interest of gaming.
